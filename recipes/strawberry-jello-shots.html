Fruity and Delectable Jello Shots

These delicious, fruity jello shots are the perfect addition to any party. Tasting like the best starburst flavor (Strawberry, of course!), these will be gone in minutes. *Disclaimer: Recommend omitting starburst garnish if swallowing whole.

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Bring water to boil in saucepan.
  2. Add Jell-o mix and let dissolve.
  3. Add vodka and cool whip. Mix together.
  4. Pour mixture into 2oz. shot cups and place in fridge for 6 hours.
  5. Garnish with pink Starburst (and let's be honest, more cool whip)
