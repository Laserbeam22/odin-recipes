Spicy Peanut Butter Noodles

Delicious Sweet and Savory Spicy Peanut Butter Noodles

Have you been craving Asian-inspired cuisine, but just don't have the budget? The ingredient list is simple and full of pantry staples you might already have. This recipe is great if you have an impromptu guest coming over and want to impress them. Even picky eaters love this meal. These delicious peanut noodles are perfect for busy-bodies who still want a home-cooked meal.

In only 15 minutes, you can sit down to a wonderful flavor profile that begs you to take a break. From the savory comfort of peanut butter, to the sweetness of maple syrup, this dish has it all. Are you a texture person? Add some chopped peanuts for a beautiful dichotomy to the soft noodles. You deserve this filling dish; read on to find out how to enjoy the moment more.

Ingredients








  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1-2 tbsp sriracha
  • 1-2 tbsp maple syrup or honey
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Optional: 2 tsp chili garlic sauce

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook noodles according to package.
  2. Mince garlic, then saute in pan with 1 tbsp of oil. (Any oil will do)
  3. Saute over low heat for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown. Add in grated ginger and saute another 1-2 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and add all remaining sauce ingredients (start with 1/3 cup of water) and whisk until smooth, adding more water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  5. Taste and add more seasonings and/or ingredients to desired taste.
  6. Add about 1/2 of the sauce to the pasta and mix. Continue adding sauce to reach desired consistency.
  7. Serve with crushed peanuts, scallions, red pepper flakes and enjoy.
