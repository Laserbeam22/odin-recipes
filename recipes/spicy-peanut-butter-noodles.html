Spicy Peanut Butter Noodles

Bowl of noodles in peanut sauce topped with peanuts and scallions

Have you been craving Asian-inspired cuisine, but just don't have the budget? The ingrediant list is simple and full of pantry staples you might already have. This recipe is great if you have an impromtu guest coming over and want to impress them. Even picky eaters love this meal. These delicious peanut noodles are perfect for busy-bodies who still want a home-cooked meal.

In only 15 minutes, you can sit down to a wonderful flavor profile that begs you to take a break. From the savory comfort of peanut butter, to the sweetness of maple syrup, this dish has it all. Are you a texture person? Add some chopped peanuts for a beautiful dichotomy to the soft noodles. You deserve this filling dish; read on to find out how to enjoy the moment more.
