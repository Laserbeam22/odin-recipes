Simple and Delicious Macaroni and Cheese

A quick and easy recipe for some delicious mac and cheese. Even the pickiest kids are sure to love this hit. Homemade mac and cheese has never tasted so good.

Ingredients













Steps

  1. Cook macaroni according to package directions. Drain and set aside afterwards.
  2. In a medium-sized saucepan, add butter and slowly melt over low heat. Add flour to melted butter and stir until well combined and mixture thickens.
  3. Turn the stove top up to medium head and slowly add the milk, around a half of a cup at a time. Continue to stir the sauce until it starts to lightly simmer and thicken. Next, add seasonings and mustard.
  4. Remove sauce from heat and add both the parmesan and the cheddar cheese to it. Stir until cheese is fully melted.
  5. Pour macaroni noodles into the cheese sauce and stir to join forces. Enjoy!
