Rice Krispie Treats

Simple and Sweet Rice Krispie Treats

This is the perfect desert for everyone. Easy to make, easy to share, and easy to eat. Nostalgic flavors make it hard not to reminisce over simpler times. Enjoy this classic treat with loved ones and share a smile.

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp salted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 6 cups mini marshmallows
  • 6 cups Rice Krispie cereal

Steps

  1. Grease a 9"x13" baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Melt butter in a large sauce pot or Dutch oven on stovetop.
  3. Stir vanilla extract and salt into the butter.
  4. Add mini marshmallows to the mixture, stirring until marshmallows have fully melted.
  5. Remove mixture from heat, and quickly stir in the Rice Krispie cereal.
  6. Spread the Rice Krispie mixture into the greased baking dish and let cool.
  7. Cut, serve, and enjoy!
