Rice Krispie Treats

This is the perfect desert for everyone. Easy to make, easy to share, and easy to eat. Nostalgic flavors make it hard not to reminisce over simpler times. Enjoy this classic treat with loved ones and share a smile.

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp salted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 6 cups mini marshmallows
  • 6 cups Rice Krispie cereal
